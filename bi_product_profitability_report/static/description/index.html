Product Profitability Report

Each organisation wants to know that which product are making good profit and which product are not, in the term of profitability for a specific duration. From the large product stock it is very hard to find out profitable product. By product profitability report app user can identify the product profitability and return rate with the filteration of Day, Week ,Month and Year.
